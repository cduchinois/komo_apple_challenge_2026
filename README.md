# KOMO

**KOMO is a private, on-device AI companion for Apple users who want to understand and protect their daily energy — without logging, streaks, dashboards, or compromising personal data.**

---

## 1. Problem

People are overwhelmed by wellbeing and productivity apps.

These apps ask users to track moods, maintain habits, check sleep scores, manage tasks, and interpret dashboards. Instead of reducing cognitive load, they often create more work.

But most people are not missing another tracker. **They are missing self-awareness.**

They do not know:

- why they feel drained
- when their energy starts dropping
- what restores them
- which small actions actually help them recharge

Apple devices already capture many signals related to energy — sleep, movement, workouts, screen time, calendar load, sitting time, HRV, heart rate, and Focus modes. But this data is fragmented across apps and rarely becomes simple, personal insight.

### 1.1 The real question

> What charges my energy, what drains it, and what should I do about it today?

---

## 2. Solution

KOMO is a private AI companion that turns passive Apple device signals into one simple daily energy experience.

KOMO runs **locally on-device** and uses data from iPhone, Apple Watch, Apple Health, Calendar, Screen Time, and Focus modes to understand the user's energy patterns.

Instead of showing another dashboard, KOMO represents your daily energy through a **living companion**.

### 2.1 Why energy

Energy is all you need — it's the foundation of your wellbeing.

If you have enough energy, you can focus, move, learn, socialize, recover, and take care of yourself. If your energy is constantly drained, every wellbeing goal becomes harder.

Most health and productivity apps focus on habits, moods, tasks, streaks, or dashboards. KOMO focuses on the **root layer underneath all of them: your daily energy.**

KOMO helps you understand where your energy comes from, where it goes, and how to protect it.

### 2.2 One unique data point: your daily energy level

Your energy level is influenced by what charges you and what drains you.

#### 2.2.1 What charges your energy

KOMO learns how your energy is charged by signals like:

- Sleep quality and duration
- Exercise and workouts
- Daily movement
- Not sitting for too long
- Walks, especially after meals or before sunset
- Focus time
- Recovery breaks
- Calmer evenings

#### 2.2.2 What drains your energy

KOMO also learns how your energy is drained by signals like:

- High screen time
- Social app usage
- Heavy calendar load
- Back-to-back meetings
- Sitting too long
- Low movement
- Poor sleep
- Stress signals from HRV and heart rate variation
- Late-night phone use

## 4. Killer Feature: Private Personal Energy Pattern Discovery

KOMO learns hidden patterns such as:

- "Your energy drops faster on days with three or more meetings before lunch."
- "A 15-minute walk after dinner usually helps you recharge."
- "Late screen time often makes your mornings feel heavier."
- "Your energy recovers better when you move before sunset."
- "Long sitting blocks usually predict lower energy in the evening."

These are insights users often do not notice themselves.

### 4.1 From insight to tiny action

KOMO turns those insights into tiny, well-timed actions:

- "A 15-minute walk after dinner usually helps you recharge." **[Add reminder]**
- "You have back-to-back meetings this morning. A 3-minute reset before the block may help protect your energy." **[Start reset]**
- "You haven't moved much today. Taking the stairs once can still give your body a small boost." **[Start mini climb]**
- "Getting off the metro one stop earlier can help you end the day with more calm." **[Remind me on commute]**

## 5. Companion Loop

KOMO is not just a score. It is a relationship.

When you protect your energy, KOMO grows with you.

### 5.1 Snacks

| Action | Snack |
| --- | --- |
| A walk | Sunberries |
| Good sleep | Dreamfruit |
| A focus session | Focus Beans |
| Lower screen time | Quiet Leaves |
| Recovery | Calm Drops |

Users can feed these snacks to KOMO. Feeding KOMO reveals what it learned about the user's energy and helps the companion grow.

### 5.2 Not a Tamagotchi

KOMO never dies, decays, or punishes absence. It grows through **self-awareness, not guilt.**

### 5.3 The product loop

```
Live your day
      ↓
KOMO reads passive Apple signals
      ↓
KOMO discovers energy patterns
      ↓
KOMO gives one insight
      ↓
KOMO suggests one tiny action
      ↓
The user protects their energy
      ↓
KOMO grows with them
```
